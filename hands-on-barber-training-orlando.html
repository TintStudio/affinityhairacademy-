Barber students at Affinity Hair Academy can complete the FAFSA online, providing details about their financial situation to see if they qualify for federal assistance. Unlike loans, grants like the Pell Grant don’t need to be repaid, making them an excellent option for reducing education costs. The school’s financial aid team can guide students through the application process to ensure they maximize available opportunities.
